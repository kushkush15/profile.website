Indian Festivals

Holi

Holi is an indian festival known as the “Festival of Colors,”. It celebrates the start of spring and good winning over evil.

People celebrate by throwing colored powders and water at each other. It starts with a bonfire and continues with music, dance, and more food.

Diwali

Diwali, is an indian festival known as the “Festival of Lights,”. It celebrates the return of an exciled king, his wife, and his brother.

People light oil lamps, decorate their homes, and set off fireworks as to celebrate and welcome people into their homes.
